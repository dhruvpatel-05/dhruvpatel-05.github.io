NBA Timeout Optimization
via Hidden Markov Models

Fit a 3-state HMM on 250K+ NBA possessions to infer latent momentum regimes from sequential scoring data. Applied within-team covariate matching across 12,000+ pairs to isolate causal timeout impact from team quality.
